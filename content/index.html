I focus on understanding the consequences of trait change due to evolutionary and plastic responses in changing enviornments. As a PhD Candidate at UC Davis, I'm working to develop theory on population dynamics resulting from these processes, and methods for connecting this theory to data. I like to think about applications, especially to salmon.

My Master's research focused on the ecological and evolutionary dynamics of sea lice and salmonids with Prof. Mark Lewis at the Centre for Mathematical Biology/Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at University of Alberta.
