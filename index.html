Jaime Ashander

rapid evolution: theory, computation, inference

I focus on understanding the consequences of trait change due to evolutionary and plastic responses in changing enviornments. As a PhD Candidate in the Baskett Lab at UC Davis, I'm working to develop theory on population dynamics resulting from these processes, and methods for connecting this theory to data. I like to think about applications, especially to salmon.

My Master's research focused on the ecological and evolutionary dynamics of sea lice and salmonids with Mark Lewis at the Centre for Mathematical Biology at University of Alberta.
